In Loving Memory Of

Edley Sylvester Gibbons

Pie & Tiger

SUNRISE 2ND DECEMBER 1932 - SUNSET 18TH AUGUST 2025 A life of faith and legacy of love

TRIBUTES

Forever in Our Hearts

Your love, strength and faith left a mark on everyone who had the privilege of knowing you. Though you are no longer here with us, the memories you gave us will remain forever.

With love, your family

A Life of Faith

You lived your life with faith in God and love for your family. We take comfort knowing that you are now at peace in the presence of the Lord. Until we meet again.

Forever remembered

Your Legacy Lives On

From Jamaica to England, you built a life through courage, hard work and determination. The family you helped build and the lessons you passed down are part of the legacy you leave behind.

Your loving family

Thank You

Thank you for the memories, the stories, the laughter and everything you taught us throughout your life. We will continue to speak your name and share your story with the generations that come after us.

With all our love

Until We Meet Again

We may not be able to see you, but you will never be forgotten. Your memory lives within your children, grandchildren and everyone whose life you touched.

Your family

Rest in the Arms of the Lord

We trust that God has welcomed you home. May you rest peacefully, knowing that you were deeply loved here on earth and will continue to be remembered every day.

Until we meet again
